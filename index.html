The Atelier

I grew up between two cities and one long Rio summer. My grandfather was a mining engineer; he came home from every journey with stones wrapped in paper, and my grandmother would sit with a goldsmith and turn them into something you could wear.

I spent those afternoons studying her collection, learning that each jewel holds a moment — a place, a person, a day worth keeping. I make my jewelry in New York now, but that is still the whole idea.

— Marina
